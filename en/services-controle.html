Inspection and preparation are not only about checking goods. They also involve anticipating risks, preparing shipments under the right conditions, and securing key steps before departure to streamline your imports from China.

✔Goods preparation

Consolidation of goods from multiple suppliers or orders to prepare a more structured and efficient shipment.

Coordination with suppliers, tracking of exchanges, and improved flow between preparation, inspection, consolidation, and shipment.
