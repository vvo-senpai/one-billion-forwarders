Detailed supplier sourcing services in China

Sourcing is not just about finding a factory. It involves selecting the right partners, verifying their reliability, and clarifying working conditions before launch to better secure your imports from China.

✔Supplier sourcing

Search for manufacturers, workshops, or partners able to meet your product, volume, and specifications. The goal is to identify suppliers aligned with your needs and quality standards.

✔Supplier verification

Review of key information, initial validation of supplier reliability, and consistency analysis before moving forward.

✔Supplier negotiation

Support in clarifying commercial and operational conditions: pricing, lead times, MOQs, negotiation margins, and key points to secure before agreement.

✔Quotation & terms validation

Review and comparison of proposals to understand differences, validate conditions, and move forward on a clearer basis.

✔Factory coordination

Follow-up with suppliers, coordination of requests, clarification of blocking points, product validation, and preparation before production to create smoother collaboration between your company and the factory.
