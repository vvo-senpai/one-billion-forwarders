Why we created One Billion Forwarders

We created One Billion Forwarders to provide a more direct, clearer, and operational support system for companies importing from China, with real expertise in supplier sourcing, factory coordination, documentation control, international transport, and customs clearance.

Rather than a purely theoretical approach, we aim to build a useful, responsive, and field-oriented structure capable of helping with supply chain challenges, import-export logistics, cargo consolidation, shipment tracking, and pre-shipment risk control.
